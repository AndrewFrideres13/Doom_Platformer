About Me

I LOVE to play with any and all kinds of technology, from the new stuff, to messing around with old languages and programs. I tend to have an old school mindset on just about everything, from music, to values, to work ethic, and I tend to be a bit stubborn, if I get handed a project I will work tirelessly to not only get it done, but get it perfected. In my spare time I like to play a wide variety of games, from DOOM to Deus Ex to the Elder Scrolls series. Otherwise, I am usually just playing with some new (to me) language or tech that I managed to get my hands on and attempting to make something useful out of it!
